    • About Me

    Brady Dittfield is a 22 year old photographer/videographer from central Indiana. However, he currently resides in Keystone, Colorado where he works at Vale Resorts. Brady Dittfield has a little over a year of experience in photography/videography. Brady also is an avid snowboarder and adventurer who loves filming his runs down the mountains in Colorado. Brady uses a mulitude of equipment to get his shots. Most notable are his Sony A7iii Camera and his DJI FPV drone All photos and logos on this website were created by Brady himself. If you would like Brady to take your pictures or videos contact him through the contact page on this website or reach out to him on his socails. Brady also does editing through Final Cut Pro and Photoshop. All pricing is subject to the scope of the job.
